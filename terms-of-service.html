2. Description of Service

IncidentApp is a mobile application designed to help users document accidents and incidents. The App provides tools for:

  • Capturing photos, videos, and audio recordings at the scene of an incident
  • Scanning and storing documents such as driver's licenses, insurance cards, and vehicle registrations using optical character recognition (OCR)
  • Recording witness and passenger information
  • Generating comprehensive incident reports
  • Timestamping and preserving evidence integrity
  • Optionally connecting users with licensed attorneys through our attorney referral service (see Section 3)

The App is currently offered on a free basis. We may introduce optional paid features or subscription tiers in the future. If we do, we will notify you and obtain your consent before charging any fees.

  • The actual content of your files is NOT stored on the blockchain ‚Äî only the hash
