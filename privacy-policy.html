Introduction

Appalanche Inc. ("Company," "we," "our," or "us") operates the IncidentApp mobile application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By downloading, installing, or using IncidentApp, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. This Privacy Policy should be read together with our Terms of Service, which govern your use of the App.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Personal identification information (name, phone number, email address)
  • Driver's license information when you scan your license
  • Vehicle and insurance information
  • Photos and videos of accident scenes
  • Voice recordings and written statements
  • Witness and passenger contact information
  • Medical treatment information you choose to log

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device location (GPS coordinates) when documenting an incident
  • Date and time stamps
  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • Weather conditions at your location
  • Nearby points of interest (for camera detection feature)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Help you document accident scenes comprehensively
  • Generate incident reports from your collected data
  • Process documents using optical character recognition (OCR)
  • Provide AI-powered analysis of your documentation
  • Connect you with legal representation if you request it through our optional attorney referral service
  • Timestamp and verify evidence authenticity
  • Improve our services and develop new features

Data Processing & AI

IncidentApp uses artificial intelligence and automated systems to help you:

  • Extract text from scanned documents such as driver's licenses, insurance cards, and vehicle registrations (OCR)
  • Parse and organize document data into structured formats
  • Analyze photos and videos for documentation quality and completeness
  • Generate incident reports and narrative summaries
  • Suggest relevant questions based on your incident type

How We Protect Your Data During AI Processing

Your privacy is built into our AI pipeline. The App uses Google Cloud services for optical character recognition (OCR) and Anthropic's Claude for document parsing. We maintain appropriate data processing agreements with these providers to ensure your data is handled in compliance with applicable privacy and security requirements.

Accuracy

AI-generated content may contain errors or inaccuracies. You are responsible for reviewing and verifying all extracted data and generated reports before relying on them. The App provides tools to edit and correct any AI-processed information.

Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of certain data processing

  • Withdraw consent at any time

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your data, and the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@appalanche.ai

Location Data

IncidentApp requests location access to document where incidents occur. Location data is used to:

  • Record the exact GPS coordinates of an accident
  • Find nearby security cameras that may have footage
  • Obtain weather conditions at your location
  • Generate accurate incident reports

Location data is only collected when you actively use incident documentation features and grant permission. You can disable location access at any time through your device settings, though this will limit some App functionality.

Camera & Photo Access

We request camera and photo library access to:

  • Capture photos and videos of accident scenes
  • Scan documents like driver's licenses and insurance cards
  • Record video statements from witnesses
  • Import dashcam footage for documentation

Photos and videos are stored locally on your device unless you choose to back them up to the cloud or submit them through the attorney referral service.

Microphone Access

We request microphone access to:

  • Record voice memos describing the incident
  • Capture audio during video recording of the accident scene
  • Record witness statements

Audio recordings are stored locally on your device and follow the same data handling practices as other content you create in the App.

Children's Privacy

IncidentApp is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete that information.

Users between 13 and 18 may use the App with parental or guardian permission. The optional attorney referral service is only available to users 18 or older, or those with a parent or legal guardian acting on their behalf.

Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Cloud-backed data is retained for as long as you maintain an account. If you request deletion of your account and cloud data, we will process that request within 30 days.

Data that has been shared with attorneys through the referral service, or hashes recorded on a blockchain, cannot be recalled or deleted by us after transmission.

International Users

IncidentApp is operated from the United States. If you are accessing the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and on this page, and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
